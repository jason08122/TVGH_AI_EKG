During the diagnostic process, an AI model can ascertain the presence of heart failure in a patient, and distinguish between systolic dysfunction and diastolic dysfunction.

Same as SDDD Diagnosis, but focus on distunguish normal patient with patient with systolic dysfunction.

HFrEF(射血率降低的心臟衰竭): heart failure with reduced ejection fraction.

判斷條件 : LVEF<=40%

HFpEF(射血率正常的心臟衰竭): heart failure with preserved ejection fraction.

判斷條件 : LVEF>50% 且以下條件滿足三項以上

  • LAE_40: 左心室直徑>=40mm
  • PH_above_35: 右心室收縮壓>=35mmHg
  • MedEa_below_7: 二尖瓣環內側組織的舒張速度<=7cm/s
  • high_LVEDP: 左心室舒張終末壓>=15mmHg
